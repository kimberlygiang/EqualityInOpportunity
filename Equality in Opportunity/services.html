About Equality in Opportunity

Equality in Opportunity is dedicated to helping children of all backgrounds, especially children undergoing hardships due to finance, to have a successful future. This website was created during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, located at the Facebook Headquarters.



"One of the most powerful tools for empowering individuals and communities is making certain that any individual who wants to receive a quality education can do so." - Christine Gregoire
