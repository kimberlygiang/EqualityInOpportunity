Scholarships

Jack Kent Cook Foundation

Must have a 3.5 GPA and demonstrate financial need. Will receive college planning support, counseling, and more. Scholarship recipients will receive $40,000 per year.

Dell Scholarship Program

For high school students who have participated in a college readiness program. Must demonstrate financial need. This is a one-time award of $20,000.

Elks National Foundation

Scholarships will be awarded based on leadership, scholarship, and financial aid. The amount for this scholarship ranges between $1,000 – $12,500 each year.

Horatio Alger Association

Applicants must demonstrate determination, integrity, and critical financial need. Receive a one-time award that ranges between $7,000 – $25,000.

Jackie Robinson Foundation

Applicants must have achieved a minimum 1000 SAT score or an ACT composite score of 21. Scholarship recipients are awarded $7,500 per year.

Ron Brown Scholarship Program

For African American high schoolers who excel academically and partcipate in their community. Applicants must demonstrate financial need. Will receive $10,000 every year.

Greenhouse Scholarship Program

Demonstrate strong interest in community, persevere through difficult circumstances, and more. The overall award amount is $20,000 split in annual installments of $5,000.

Questbridge National College Match

From household earning less than $65,000 a year and experienced long-term economic hardship. This scholarship is a one-time award of $200,000.

Gordan A. Rich Memorial Foundation

Parent/guardian works in the financial services industry. Student ranks in the top 20% of their class and holds 3.5 GPA. The award amount is $12,500 per year.
