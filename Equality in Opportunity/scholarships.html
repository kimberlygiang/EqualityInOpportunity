Still looking for scholarships?

If you are still looking for scholaships that are more applicable to you, try using a free scholarship matching service such as fastweb.com . Once you sign up, Fastweb will send you emails updating you on various scholarships that match your personal background profile.

Professional Online Image

Google your name and make sure you have a professional online profile. Use a professional email address, such as firstname.lastname@gmail.com. Clean up the content of your Facebook account, removing inappropriate and immature material. Scholarship committees usually do background checks, so you want to be prepared.

Having trouble?

If you have difficulty writing essays, try recording yourself as you answer the question out loud, and transcribe the recording later. Most people can think and speak faster than they can write or type. Create an outline afterward to organize your thoughts. Remember to write about a topic that interests you.

Apply, Apply, Apply

Apply for every scholarship for which you are eligible. You have a larger chance of getting a scholarship if you apply for as many as you can. If possible, try to pursue smaller and less competitive scholarships, such as small awards and essay contests, since they are easier to win.
